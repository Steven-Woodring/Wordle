Where to Start?

The Best Opening Guesses


If you're in the camp that takes this quick-hitting daily game super seriously, then you're gonna need your very own starting word. This word is your security blanket, an instant provider of yellow and green squares that will ensure your streak persists until the sun stops burning (or at least until the 5-letter word list runs out).

Aside from the obvious requirements of no repeating letters and plenty of vowels, the best starting guesses have two things going for them. First and foremost, their letters appear very frequently in the other 12,946 words from Wordle's list of acceptable guesses. Secondly, the positioning of their letters is common, heightening their chances to produce the sacred green square right off the bat.

There are a handful of words that you can comfortably put in your first-guess holster. All of the words in the chart to the right should light up your first row fairly consistently with vibrant shades of yellows and greens.

Here's a secret: Choose one that you recognize, it has a better chance of being the solution one day. When that day comes, your friends will never hear the end of it.
